AZACCA ® BRAND
ADHA 483


Azacca®, named for the Haitian god of agriculture, is a hop big in citrus and tropical fruit
tones. It’s excellent aromatic qualities have quickly made Azacca® a go-to hop for late
and dry hop additions in varying styles of beer.
