MOTUEKA ™ BRAND
87.14-20

Developed by the New Zealand Institute for Plant and Food Research hop breeding program
and released in 1996, Motueka™ is a cross between a New Zealand breeding selection
and Saaz. It offers a unique aroma and flavor profile suitable for producing big beer styles.
Motueka™ performs well when used in multiple additions from a single hop bill.
