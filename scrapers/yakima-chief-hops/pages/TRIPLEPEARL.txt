TRIPLEPEARL
Released by USDA-ARS in 2013, TriplePearl is an open pollinated cross between a
tetraploid Perle female and an unknown diploid male. Its lineage includes Northern
Brewer and Hallertau. TriplePearl is similar to Perle but features more pronounced aroma
characteristics.
