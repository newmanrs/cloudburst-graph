COLUMBIA
Selected in 1967 at Oregon State University in Corvallis, Oregon, Columbia is a
descendant of Fuggle and sister selection of Willamette. Commercial production was
discontinued in the early 1980s in favor of Willamette, however small quantities are again
being produced.
