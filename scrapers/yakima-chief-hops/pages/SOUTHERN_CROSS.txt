SOUTHERN CROSS
Developed by the New Zealand Institute for Plant and Food Research hop breeding program and
released in 1994, Southern Cross is a triploid cross between New Zealand Smoothcone and a 1950’s
research variety bred from a crossing of Californian and English Fuggle. It has excellent essential oils
and low co-humulone delivering a delicate balance of citrus and spice when added at the end of boil.
