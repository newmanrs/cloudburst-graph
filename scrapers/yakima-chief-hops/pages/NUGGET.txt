NUGGET
Bred in 1970 by the USDA breeding program in Oregon and released in 1983, Nugget is a
high alpha cultivar that is beginning to gain some acceptance as a dual purpose variety.
Its lineage includes Brewer’s Gold, Canterbury Golding and Early Green.
