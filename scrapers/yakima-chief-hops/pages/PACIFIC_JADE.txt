PACIFIC JADE™ BRAND
HORT1524

Developed by the New Zealand Institute for Plant and Food Research hop breeding program and
released in 2004, Pacific Jade™ is a cross of New Zealand First Choice (a relative of the Late Cluster)
and a Saaz male. It is suited for use as a bittering hop but also delivers bold citrus and spice aroma
characteristics.
