CRYSTAL
Bred in 1983 by the USDA, Crystal is a triploid aroma-type cultivar from Hallertau Mittelfrüh,
Cascade, Brewer’s Gold and Early Green. It is primarily grown in Oregon and has become
increasingly popular among craft brewers due to its versatility in a variety of beer styles.
