NEWPORT
Bred in 1992 by Oregon State University and released in 1992 through the USDA, Newport
is a descendant of the classic bittering variety, Magnum. It contains high alpha acid,
co-humulone and myrcene content, offering more distinct aroma characteristics than its
parent.
