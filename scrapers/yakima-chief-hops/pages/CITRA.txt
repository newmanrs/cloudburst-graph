CITRA ® BRAND
HBC 394 CV


Developed by Hop Breeding Company and released in 2007, Citra® HBC 394 cv. features high
alpha acid and total oil content with a low percentage of co-humulone. It is one of the top
ten aroma varieties for craft brewers imparting distinct citrus and tropical fruit flavors.
