SUMMER ™ BRAND
Bred in 1997 at Tasmanian Bushy Park Breeding Garden, Summer™ is the result of open
pollination of a tetraploid Czech Saaz. It features balanced citrus and stone fruit flavors,
but showcases distinct apricot and melon characteristics in dry hopping applications.
