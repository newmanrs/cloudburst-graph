FALCONER’S FLIGHT ®
BLEND


Developed by Hopunion LLC in 2010, Falconer’s Flight® hop pellets are an exclusive proprietary hop
blend created to honor and support the legacy of Northwest brewing legend, Glen Hay Falconer.
Proceeds from each Falconer’s Flight® purchase is contributed to the Glen Hay Falconer Foundation.
These hop pellets are an excellent complement to many IPA and Pale Ale-oriented hop varieties.
Specific aroma descriptors include distinct tropical, floral, lemon and grapefruit characteristics.
