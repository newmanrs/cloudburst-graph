PACIFIC GEM
Developed by the New Zealand Institute for Plant and Food Research hop breeding program and
released in 1987, Pacific Gem is a triploid alpha variety from New Zealand Smoothcone crossed with
Californian Late Cluster and Fuggle. It is typically used as a first hop addition for bittering purposes,
but is known for showcasing wood and blackberry flavors.
