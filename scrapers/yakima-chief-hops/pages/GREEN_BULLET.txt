GREEN BULLET
Released in 1972 from the New Zealand DSIR (now New Zealand Institute for Plant and
Food Research) Green Bullet is a triploid alpha variety bred by open pollination of the
New Zealand “Smoothcone” variety. It is traditionally considered a bittering variety for
lagers, but also carries a spicy characteristics typical of Slovenia hop varieties.
