ZYTHOS ®
BLEND


Named in honor of the Greek word for beer, Zythos® was designed to complement existing IPA and
Pale Ale hop varieties. It is an excellent blend for any hop forward beer. Specific aroma descriptors
include distinct tropical (pineapple) and citrus tones with slight pine characteristics.
