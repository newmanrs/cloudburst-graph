PALISADE ® BRAND
YCR 4 CV


Developed by Yakima Chief Ranches, Palisade® YCR 4 cv. is known for its high yield and
unique aroma profile. With moderate alpha acid, Palisade® borders on being a dual
purpose hop, however, more complex characteristics are seen in later additions.
