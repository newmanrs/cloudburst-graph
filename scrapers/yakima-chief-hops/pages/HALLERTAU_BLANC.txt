HALLERTAU BLANC
Bred at the Hop Research Institute in Hüll and released in 2012, Hallertau Blanc was
commercialized as a new, German aroma variety in response to growing demand from the
craft beer industry for distinct flavor profiles. Hallertau Blanc is the daughter of Cascade
and displays a wide variety of tropical fruit, stone fruit and citrus flavors.
