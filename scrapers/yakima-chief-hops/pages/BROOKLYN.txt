BROOKLYN
Developed by the New Zealand Institute for Plant and Food Research hop breeding program and
released in 2015, Brooklyn is a triploid variety from New Zealand Southern Cross and a selected
New Zealand male. It is a big hop with high alpha acid content, however, sensory panels have also
indicated the presence of grapefruit, tropical fruit and passionfruit characteristics.
