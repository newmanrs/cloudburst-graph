TOPAZ ™ BRAND
TC-85-70

Bred during the 1980s, Topaz™ did not find popularity within the brewing community until
2007-2008. Its ancestry includes an existing high alpha Australian variety crossed with a
Wye College male. Topaz™ is known for its single hop applications, producing resinous,
grass and tropical fruit flavors.
