KOHATU ® BRAND
Developed by the New Zealand Institute for Plant and Food Research hop breeding
program and released in 2011, Kohatu® is a descendant of Hallertau Mittelfrüh. It works
well in single hop applications and is described as having intense fruit characteristics.
