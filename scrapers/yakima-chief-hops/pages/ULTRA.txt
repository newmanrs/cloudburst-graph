ULTRA
Bred by the hops research program in Corvallis, Oregon in 1983 and released in 1995 by
the USDA, Ultra is a triploid seedling of Hallertau Mittelfrüh and half sister to Mt. Hood,
Liberty and Crystal. Ultra is related to traditional German varieties and can be utilized in
similar applications.
