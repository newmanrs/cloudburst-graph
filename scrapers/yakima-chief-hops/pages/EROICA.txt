EROICA
Released by the Idaho Agricultural Experiment Station, Oregon Agricultural Experiment
Station, and USDA-ARS in 1982, Eroica is an open pollinated cross of Brewer’s Gold. It is
primarily a bittering hop, however some brewers have noted fruit forward flavors in late
applications.
