SLÁDEK
A hybrid aroma variety of Saaz-type and Northern Brewer, Sládek displays noble
characteristics with a fruity twist. Its name is derived from the Czech word meaning “beer
brewer.” Sládek is noted to be a good complement to Saaz in late-hopping applications.
