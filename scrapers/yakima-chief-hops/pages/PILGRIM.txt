PILGRIM
Bred at Wye College in the late 1990s and released in 2001, Pilgrim is half-sister to First
Gold and sister to Herald. It is often compared to Target. Pilgrim’s selinene content helps
enhance brewing performance and produce a “hoppier” aroma.
