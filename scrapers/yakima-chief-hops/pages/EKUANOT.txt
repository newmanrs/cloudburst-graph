EKUANOT ® BRAND
HBC 366 CV

Developed by Hop Breeding Company and released in 2014, Ekuanot® HBC 366 cv.
features pronounced aroma characteristics and extremely high oil content. This variety
bursts out of the spring soil in vibrant yellow and gradually matures to a deep green color
by Fall harvest.
