WAKATU ™ BRAND
77-05

Developed by the New Zealand Institute for Plant and Food Research hop breeding
program and released in 1988, as Hallertau Aroma, Wakatu™ was renamed in 2011. It is a
triplod variety with two thirds Hallertau Mittelfrüh parentage. Wakatu™ is a well rounded
variety with strong, fresh citrus characteristics and some bittering capabilities.
